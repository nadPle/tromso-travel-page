Why Tromsø?

Tromsø - worth a trip up north!

During a trip to Scandinavia with my hubby in the summer months of 2016 I fell in love with Tromsø, where the sun dances at midnight! Nestled at 69° North, this Arctic gem in Northern Norway is surrounded by breathtaking mountains, serene fjords, and delightful island getaways. In summer, it's a land where the sun never takes a break. The unspoiled Arctic wilderness is just a stone's throw away from the city's vibrant culture.

From nature's embrace by day to the city's lively beat by night, it's all seamlessly woven together there. You can dive into a kaleidoscope of experiences, whether you're chasing nature's wonders or savoring the local culture, food, and the pulsating nightlife. With a great variety of activities, there's something to delight every adventurer!
